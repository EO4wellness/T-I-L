# Terms:
* plysovy medvedik (teddy bear)
* babika (doll)
* hracka (toy)
* pocitac (computer)

# Sentences 
* Kde su hracky?  Where are the toys
* Ja chcem kupit plysoveho medvedika a babiku. I want to buy a teddy bear and a doll.
* Ja potrebujem pocitac. I need a computer.
