Tribute to Sir Isaac Newton

The content of this page is a tribute page to accomplishments of Sir Isaac Newton who was a really cool guy who wrote about and observed the world around him in all its splendor.

Timeline

  • December 25, 1642: Born in Woolsthorpe Manor House, United Kingdom
  • 1655-1659: King's School
  • 1661-1665: Attended Trinity College
  • 1667-1669: Attended Trinity College
  • 1703: Elected President of the Royal Society.
  • 1704: Publishes Opticks: Treatis of the Reflexions, Refractions, Inflexions and Colours of Light.
  • 1705: Queen Anne knights Newton, the first time a scientist receives the honor.
  • March 31, 1727: Died in Kensington
